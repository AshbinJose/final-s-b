Construction Services

At S & B Constructions, we offer a complete range of professional construction services designed to turn your vision into reality with quality, precision, and reliability. Our expertise spans across building construction, residential housing projects, commercial developments, and all types of civil engineering works. From planning and designing to execution and finishing, we deliver projects that meet the highest standards of durability and functionality.

We undertake housing construction, ensuring comfortable, safe, and modern living spaces tailored to client requirements. Our building construction services cover everything from small-scale structures to large commercial and institutional projects. We also specialize in PWD works, handling government-approved projects with strict compliance to safety codes and regulations. In addition, our team provides maintenance and renovation services, helping extend the life of existing buildings and giving them a fresh, modern appeal. Whether it is structural repairs, interior upgrades, or general upkeep, we deliver hassle-free solutions.

With a skilled workforce, advanced technology, and a commitment to timely delivery, we cater to every kind of construction requirement. At S & B Constructions, we don’t just build structures—we build trust, long-lasting value, and spaces that stand strong for generations.

Fabrication Services

S & B Constructions delivers high-quality fabrication services designed to meet the diverse needs of residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our expertise covers steel, aluminum, and other metal works, crafted with precision engineering and modern techniques. From heavy structural fabrication for warehouses and buildings to custom gates, grills, staircases, and roofing solutions, our products are built for strength, durability, and design appeal.

We manage industrial fabrication projects with a focus on safety, performance, and cost efficiency. Our custom fabrication services include sheet metal works, welded assemblies, and on-site installations tailored to each client’s unique requirements. To ensure long-term value, we also provide maintenance and repair services for existing fabricated structures. Every project reflects our commitment to fine finishing, structural reliability, and on-time delivery.

For us, fabrication is more than working with metal—it is about creating lasting strength, enhancing functionality, and delivering solutions that balance utility with style. Whatever the scale of your requirement, S & B Constructions ensures fabrication services that are dependable, efficient, and built to last.
